My Story

At age 13, after struggling to teach myself C++, I found success building a videogame-centered website. After studying music in college, I worked as musician and teacher, and did part-time public/human services work. In 2017 I decided to pivot to software development as a career, and began studies at Prime Digital Academy. Since then I have focused on building full-stack web applications, and enjoy discussing the many philosophical and logical ways music relates to technology.

As a professional musician, one has many challenges, and is required to spend many hours practicing skills with a self-critical eye, supportively communicating ideas with other musicians, analyzing every technical, artistic, and emotional element of a musical piece, and achieving the best public performance possible.
