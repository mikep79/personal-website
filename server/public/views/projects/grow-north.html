Why we built it:

  • App gives client Grow North MN a more efficient way to manage professional connections made in the food startup community.

What it does:

  • Mobile-responsive survey invites business owner's to add their information to Grow North's network.
  • Search function allows client to easily find businesses and people in directory.
  • Connections can be made between each business, and comments added on meetings or developments.
  • All data can be conveniently edited.

This app was built by team GeckMN using AngularJS, Node.js, Express, PostgresSQL, and AngularJS Material.
